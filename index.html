    My name is Yizhou Shan . I’m a second-year Ph.D. student at Purdue ECE, advised by Prof. Yiying Zhang. Our lab is Wuklab.io. Yes, the Monkey King Lab. Please find my CV here.

    What’s up

    • [Jul 2018] Our paper Lego: Disaggregated Operating System was accepted to OSDI‘18
    • [May 2018] Intern at VMware Research, with Stanko Novakovic.

    Research

    My research interests span Operating System, Distributed Systems, and Non-Volatile Memory. I like hacking kernel and no doubt Linux is my favorite open-source project.
