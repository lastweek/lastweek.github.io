    Research¶

    During my doctoral research, I have been working on distributed system, networking, operating system, hardware (FPGA), and their intersections. My most recent research focus is on disaggregated datacenter, designing its hardware, OS, and upper layer applications.

    All of my projects are open source at https://github.com/lastweek and https://github.com/WukLab.
